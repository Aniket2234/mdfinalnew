What programs does Malay Damania offer?

Malay offers three signature coaching programs: CA Scale-Up Program (₹11,999) for chartered accountants, Finance for Non-Finance Program (₹11,999) for business owners, and Business Mastery Program (₹14,999) for SME owners facing growth challenges.

Who is the CA Scale-Up Program for?

This program is specifically designed for practicing Chartered Accountants and Company Secretaries who are stuck at limited growth, dependent only on referrals, and want to scale beyond personal networks with robust client acquisition strategies.

What restaurant courses are available?

Two specialized restaurant courses: Restaurant Menu course (₹127) - a 40-minute session on strategic menu crafting, and Restaurant Growth Course (₹1,799) - a 5-hour comprehensive guide covering all restaurant operations.

What is the Business Champ book about?

Business Champ (₹499) is a comprehensive book condensing 30+ years of real business experience into actionable tools and frameworks, providing a step-by-step roadmap from vision setting to execution with no theoretical fluff.

What is Voice of Books membership?

Voice of Books Annual Membership (₹4,999) provides condensed summaries of top business books with actionable lessons in every session, helping you gain CEO-level insights without spending hours reading.

What makes the Finance for Non-Finance Program unique?

This program simplifies financial literacy for non-finance backgrounds, provides hands-on templates and tools, and explains real-world case studies step-by-step to help entrepreneurs make smarter business decisions independently.

What challenges does the Business Mastery Program solve?

It addresses plateaued revenue and profits, ineffective marketing and sales, and operational inefficiencies through complete business audit methodology and customized growth strategies for sustainable long-term growth.

How does the Restaurant Menu course help?

It teaches strategic menu crafting that drives customer choices using placement, combos, decoy pricing, and upsells effectively. The 40-minute session is packed with actionable insights backed by real restaurant success stories.

What topics does the Business Veda Podcast cover?

The podcast features short, impactful episodes focused on business growth, financial literacy, leadership & team management, with real stories, actionable advice, and expert guests sharing their experiences.

Are there different program formats available?

Yes, Malay offers business coaching & mentoring, online group coaching programs, specialized courses, books, podcast content, and membership programs to cater to diverse learning preferences and business needs.
