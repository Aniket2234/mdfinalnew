Restaurant Menu Strategy - Craft Strategic Menus

  • Restaurant Owners Enrolled

Transform your restaurant's profitability with our strategic Restaurant Menu Strategy course designed specifically for restaurant owners who want to drive customer choices and maximize revenue. This simple 40-minute session is packed with actionable insights and proven techniques backed by real restaurant success stories. Learn to craft strategic menus using placement, combos, decoy pricing, and upsells effectively - ideal for both new and established restaurant owners looking to optimize their menu performance.

Course Requirements

  • Restaurant owners or managers with menu control
  • Food service entrepreneurs wanting to optimize pricing
  • Willingness to implement strategic menu changes
  • Access to current menu for practical application

Who This Course Is For

  • New restaurant owners designing their first strategic menu
  • Established restaurant owners wanting to increase profitability
  • Restaurant managers seeking to drive customer choices
  • Food service entrepreneurs struggling with menu pricing
  • Restaurant owners wanting to maximize revenue per customer

What You'll Learn

  • Strategic Menu Crafting: Learn to craft strategic menus that drive customer choices and influence purchasing decisions
  • Effective Placement Techniques: Master menu placement strategies that highlight high-profit items and guide customer selection
  • Combo & Decoy Pricing: Use combos, decoy pricing, and psychological pricing techniques to increase average order value
  • Upselling Strategies: Implement effective upselling techniques through strategic menu design and item positioning
